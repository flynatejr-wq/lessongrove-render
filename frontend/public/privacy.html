Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What We Collect

When you use LessonGrove, we collect:

  • Account information (email address, name) when you register
  • Teaching profile data you enter (user type, subject, student level)
  • Content you upload or paste for lesson generation
  • Generated lesson plans and curriculum schedules
  • Basic usage data (which features you use, error logs)

2. How We Use It

We use your data solely to provide and improve LessonGrove:

  • Generating lesson plans and curricula from your uploaded content
  • Personalizing output based on your teaching profile
  • Maintaining your account and lesson history
  • Diagnosing errors and improving the service

3. What We Don't Do

We do not sell your data. We do not share your uploaded content or generated lessons with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

5. Data Retention

Lesson history is stored locally in your browser. Account data is retained until you delete your account. You can delete your account at any time from Settings, which removes your account data from our systems.

6. Security

We use HTTPS, JWT-authenticated API endpoints, and industry-standard practices to protect your data. No system is perfectly secure — do not upload highly sensitive personal data.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us or using the account deletion feature in Settings.

8. Cookies

LessonGrove uses browser localStorage to store your preferences and lesson history locally on your device. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or via an in-app notice.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@lessongrove.com.
